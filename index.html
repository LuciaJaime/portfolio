COFFEETIMER

CoffeeTimer will allow the company to organize and control the schedule of a coffee shop staff, automating the salary calculation.

The Process

I started with a detailed user research, conducting interviews and case analysis. I created personas and scenarios to better understand the needs. During ideation and prototyping sessions, I generated solutions and received feedback. Usability tests confirmed the effectiveness of the design, followed by iterations to enhance it. The project is currently in the design phase and will soon transition to the development stage.

The Results and Next Steps

Administrator Views: I've optimized admin interfaces for efficient employee record management and simplified attendance tracking, streamlining the salary calculation process. You can explore the project on Figma!

Next Steps - Employee Workflow: Shifting my focus to craft an intuitive experience for employees, I'm designing user-friendly features for seamless registrations, entry/exit logs, and salary access. Stay tuned for further enhancements.
